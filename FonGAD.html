Mission Statement
We exist to educate, uplift and enrich the people of Bainet by developing the community, protect the environment and built a brighter future.

Engaging our community to keep people inspired by and active in our work. Raise awareness of community development to educate people on our cause. Advocating on our issues to change hearts and minds by positioning our staff as thought leaders or experts. We communicate internally with our staff and board. FonGAD recruits and engages participants to use our programs and services. We build our membership through recruit and serving members of our organization. The organizations will recruit and engage volunteers to help deliver our programs and services.
