We are a compassionate agency innovating a robust approach to development in Gandou, Bainet.

Our team of committed and dedicated visionaries are trusted partners who facilitate community-based strategic planning efforts, provide transparency and solidarity and adhere to world best-practices standards.

FonGAD also works to secure private and public funds and serves as the collaborative applicant for the Bainet Continuum of Care, ensuring our residents have access to funding.
